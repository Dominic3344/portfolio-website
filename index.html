01 // about

Who I Am

I'm Dominic Tojan, a Second-year Computer Science student at CCE College IJK, Kerala. My lab is wherever I have a breadboard, a laptop, and strong enough WiFi to flash firmware over-the-air.

I started with app development, building small projects like a BMI calculator and dice‑rolling app before moving into Android apps with Java and Kotlin. Over time, I learned to connect backend logic with real‑world usability, creating mobile solutions that integrate with hardware.

My curiosity then expanded into IoT, where a simple LED circuit grew into full systems using Arduino and ESP32 — from smart medicine boxes to sustainability trackers.

Recently, I’ve been exploring game development with Godot and C#, experimenting with immersive 3D environments and interactive mechanics. For me, game dev is storytelling through code, blending creativity and logic. Whether it’s apps, IoT prototypes, or games, I thrive on turning ideas into tangible creations.
